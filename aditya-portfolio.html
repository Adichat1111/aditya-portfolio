Things I've

PROJECT 01 · FEATURED

Autonomous Stair-Climbing Robot

Designed a lobe-shaped differential wheel robot for autonomous stair climbing. Built a full SolidWorks model with URDF conversion, integrated camera and LiDAR sensors for autonomous obstacle avoidance. The robot successfully navigated multi-step stair environments in simulation.

SolidWorks ROS Gazebo URDF LiDAR
PROJECT 02

VR Safety Training for 3D Printer Operation

Developed an immersive VR training module for Stratasys 3D printers. Simulates operational hazards and safety protocols. High-fidelity 3D assets optimized for real-time XR performance on Meta Quest 3.

Unity SolidWorks Blender Meta Quest 3
PROJECT 03

Dryer Door Push Latch Mechanism

Topology-optimized push-lock/push-release dryer latch designed for high-temperature use and ≥8,000 duty cycles. Prototyped via FDM in ABS — optimized for low weight, easy assembly, and cost efficiency.

SolidWorks FDM / ABS Topology Opt.
PROJECT 04

Mechatronic System Simulation — Soil Moisture

Simulated soil moisture dynamics comparing bare vs. mulched soil. Results showed approximately 20% greater moisture retention in mulched soil, informing smart irrigation system design.

MATLAB Hydrus 2D Simulink

Designed and fabricated a single-piston floating caliper for an All Terrain Vehicle. Modular Al 7075-T6 caliper reduced thermal deformation, machining cost, and displacement by 8.56% vs. conventional design.

SolidWorks ANSYS Al 7075-T6
